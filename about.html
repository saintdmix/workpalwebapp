Our Story

Built for the People
Who Build Things

WorkPal was created to bridge the gap between skilled artisans and the clients who need them — making it easier to find work, get paid, and grow a professional reputation in Nigeria and beyond.

Our Mission

Empowering Every Artisan

We believe every skilled person deserves a professional platform — not just the ones with connections. WorkPal gives electricians, plumbers, tailors, photographers, developers, and hundreds of other professionals the tools to showcase their work, connect with clients, and build a sustainable business.

Whether you're a customer looking for reliable help or an artisan ready to grow, WorkPal is your home.
